# Safari Web Apps Setup

These apps are created manually using Safari's "Add to Dock" feature. They appear as native-like apps in the Dock and in `~/Applications/`, but are actually website shortcuts.

## Apps to Create

### 1. CLZ Comics

1. Open Safari
2. Navigate to: https://app.clz.com/comics
3. Click the **Share** button (top-right)
4. Click **Add to Dock**
5. Name it: `CLZ Comics`
6. Click **Add**

The app will appear in `~/Applications/CLZ Comics.app`

### 2. CLZ Books

1. Open Safari
2. Navigate to: https://app.clz.com/books
3. Click the **Share** button (top-right)
4. Click **Add to Dock**
5. Name it: `CLZ Books`
6. Click **Add**

The app will appear in `~/Applications/CLZ Books.app`

---

## Notes

- These apps are stored in your home directory's `~/Applications/` folder, not the system `/Applications/`
- They launch Safari in a dedicated window without address bar or tabs
- To update or remove them, delete the `.app` from `~/Applications/` and recreate if needed
